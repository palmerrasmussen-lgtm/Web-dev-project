Why I Like Golf

What I like most about golf is that it mixes patience, skill, and strategy. A good round is not just about power. It is also about staying calm, making smart decisions, and being consistent. Golf is also fun because you can play competitively or just enjoy being outside with friends.

How to Get Better at Golf

One of the best parts of golf is that there are a lot of different ways to improve. Here is a simple improvement plan:

  1. Build strong fundamentals
    • Practice your grip
    • Focus on alignment
    • Keep a balanced stance
  2. Spend time on short game
    • Work on chipping
    • Practice putting distance control
    • Learn basic bunker shots
  3. Practice with purpose
    • Do not just hit random balls at the range
    • Set goals for each practice session
    • Track what part of your game needs the most help
