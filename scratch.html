The Masters Tournament

The Masters stands out because of Augusta National, the green jacket, and the history that seems to show up every April. It feels different from every other golf event, and that is exactly why it matters so much.

2025 Champion Moment

Rory McIlroy’s win at the 2025 Masters added another unforgettable chapter to the event’s history. The tournament always feels larger than one weekend because every winner becomes part of a tradition that fans remember for years.

Why the Masters Is Special

Part of what makes the Masters different is that it blends competition with tradition in a way no other tournament really does.

  1. Traditions
    • Green Jacket ceremony
    • Champions Dinner
    • Par 3 Contest
  2. Course identity
    • Amen Corner
    • Rae’s Creek
    • Azaleas and iconic scenery
  3. History
    • Legendary champions
    • Sunday pressure
    • Moments fans remember for decades
